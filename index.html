What makes Nivare different

Built to understand you

Not just another chatbot. Nivare remembers your story, detects your mood, and adapts in real time.

🧠

Mood-Aware AI

Nivare detects your emotional state through conversation patterns and adapts its responses — supportive when you need comfort, energetic when you're up for it.

🎵

Spotify Mood Detection

Connect Spotify and let Nivare auto-play playlists that match your current emotions. Feeling anxious? It plays calming lo-fi. Feeling great? Upbeat vibes.

🎙

Voice Interaction

Wake words, speech-to-text, and natural voice responses. Talk to Nivare like you'd talk to a friend — hands-free, anytime.

❤

Memory & Context

Nivare remembers your conversations, builds a knowledge graph of what matters to you, and uses it to give genuinely personalised responses.

🛡

Wellbeing & Coping Tools

Self-guided journaling, thought records, and grounding exercises inspired by widely-used wellbeing techniques. A space for reflection — not a replacement for professional care.

🧠

ADHD Focus Tools

Energy-based suggestions, daily action icons that disappear as you complete them, adaptive chat length, and dopamine reward streaks — built for neurodivergent brains.

💰

Financial Impulse Tracker

Log spending urges, celebrate when you resist, track your resist rate and money saved. Gamified accountability for impulsive spending.

🧘

Focus Together

A silent "body-double" focus session — Nivare sits with you while you work. A calm timer, gentle presence cues, zero pressure. Stopping early still counts.

🎵

Play Any Song

Just ask — "play Sidhu Moose Wala" or "play sad Punjabi songs" — in any language. Nivare finds it and opens it in Spotify. Music in Hindi, Punjabi, English and more.

🔐

Private & Secure

Your sensitive data — moods, focus, impulse logs — stays on your device. No data selling, no ads. You can delete everything at any time.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Nivare ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Nivare mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and display name through Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In).

Conversation Data: Messages you send to the AI companion are processed to generate responses. Conversations may be stored locally on your device and in our secure cloud database to enable features like memory and personalisation.

Mood & Emotion Data: Nivare analyses conversational cues to detect your emotional state. This data is used solely to improve your experience (e.g., suggesting music, adapting responses). It is never sold or shared with advertisers.

Voice Data: When you use voice features, audio is processed for speech-to-text conversion. We do not store raw audio recordings.

Spotify Data: If you connect Spotify, we access your playback controls and playlist data through Spotify's API. We do not store your Spotify credentials.

Energy & ADHD Data: If you use the ADHD focus tools, we store your daily energy level selections, action completion status, dopamine streak data, and impulse tracker entries locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

Financial Impulse Data: Spending urge descriptions and amounts you log in the impulse tracker are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your bank accounts, transactions, or financial information.

Usage Analytics: We collect anonymised usage data (feature usage, crash reports) through Firebase Analytics to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the AI companion experience
  • To detect mood and suggest appropriate music or coping strategies
  • To provide energy-based suggestions and ADHD focus tools
  • To track impulse spending patterns locally for your self-awareness
  • To maintain your conversation history and memory graph
  • To process subscription payments through Apple/Google
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To send important service notifications

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with encryption in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices including:

  • TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Firebase Security Rules to restrict data access
  • Secure token-based authentication
  • No plaintext storage of sensitive credentials

4. Third-Party Services

Nivare uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management
  • Spotify — Music playback (optional, user-initiated)
  • AI Language Models — Conversation processing (no personal data is used for model training)

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers. We only share data with third-party services as described above, solely to provide the app's functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your conversation history
  • Opt out of analytics data collection
  • Disconnect third-party integrations (Spotify) at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@nivare.app.

7. Children's Privacy

Nivare is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of Nivare after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:
